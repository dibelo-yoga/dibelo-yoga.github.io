Our Yoga Programs

You can have a variety of classes like Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga, Rocket Yoga, Vinyasa Flow, Aerial Yoga Flow, Pranayama (breathing control exersices), Meditation & Ayurvedic massage.

Ashtanga Vinyasa Yoga

Flow Yoga System That Combines Breath, Movement & Bhandas

Rocket Yoga

Yoga System That Combines The 3 First Series of Ashtanga Yoga

Vinyasa Flow Yoga

Flow Yoga System that every time has a different theme. Can be slow move and floating as well.

Hatha Yoga

Yoga System that you can start your yoga journey soft with long holding on the Asanas.

Aerial Yoga

Flow Athletic Yoga for the lovers of acrobatics and for those that wants something different. .

Pranayama

Yoga Exercises to develop and expand breathing. Internal work on the lungs.

Medidation

Ayurvedic Massage

Our Studio

The Hatha Yoga & Pilates Studio is a new alternative space which combines the philosophy of Zen and today. Located just 10 minutes from the center of Patras (Greece), is located in a quiet green neighborhood above the Marina of Patras in Pelekaneika. The Hatha Studio is an island of peace and tranquility wanting to provide its guests a break from the hectic city life. Our place is made with love and care, with warm colors, emitting positivity and tranquility to every visitor. The way our teaching, cultivates and encourages trainees to hear their inner teacher, without being encouraged competitive environment in the room to avoid injuries, which is our main concern.
